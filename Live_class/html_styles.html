Syed Ali Geelani was born in 1929 in a village called Zurimanj,

in the Bandipora tehsil, in the Baramulla district of North Kashmir.

He was the son of a landless labourer in the canals department.

Geelani was educated partly in Sopore (his ancestral village) and the rest in Lahore. He studied in a madrasa attached to the Masjid Wazir Khan and later enrolled in the Oriental College.

He completed Adib 'Alim, a course in Islamic theology
